  3. it is not

Write full sentences. Use is/isn't/are/aren't.

  1. (your shoes very dirty)
  2. (my brother a teacher)

  4. (the shops not open today)
  5. (my keys in my bag)

  5. (near here / the post office?)
  6. (at school / your children?)
  7. (why / you / late?)
